Everything you need to know about NairaBank

What is NairaBank?
NairaBank is a digital banking platform that lets you send money, create virtual cards, track your spending, and save money — all from one app. We're building banking that actually works for Nigerians.
Is NairaBank safe to use?
Absolutely. We use bank-grade 256-bit encryption, biometric authentication, and real-time fraud detection. Your money and data are protected at all times.
How do I open an account?
Download the NairaBank app from the App Store or Google Play, enter your phone number, verify your BVN, and you're ready to go. Takes less than 5 minutes.
Are there any hidden fees?
No. We believe in transparent banking. What you see is what you get. No surprise charges, ever.
Can I get a physical debit card?
Yes! Every NairaBank account comes with a free physical debit card delivered to your doorstep. You can also create virtual cards instantly for online shopping.
